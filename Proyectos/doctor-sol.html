Project Journey

Challenge

Launch Dr. Sol in Peru where customer behavior differed significantly from Colombia and Mexico. We needed to establish brand presence and drive adoption in a market with unique characteristics and preferences, while competing with established financial institutions.

Market Research

Conducted an in-depth analysis of competitors and loan adoption behavior in Peru:

  • Identified that Peruvian customers responded more to mass media than purely digital campaigns, unlike our other markets.
  • Analyzed competitor strategies and market positioning to identify gaps and opportunities.
  • Mapped the customer journey specific to Peruvian borrowers to understand decision triggers.

Strategy & Execution

  • Hybrid Approach: Designed and implemented a strategy combining ATL marketing (radio, TV, newspapers) with digital ads (Google & Facebook).
  • Integrated Campaigns: Created cohesive messaging that maintained brand consistency across all channels while addressing local market nuances.
  • Channel Synergy: Ensured offline and online channels reinforced each other, with ATL driving branded searches and digital campaigns capturing that interest.
  • SEO Leverage: Capitalized on ATL exposure which generated branded searches and media mentions, accelerating domain authority and organic traffic growth.

Results

  • Successfully launched operations in Peru, achieving faster growth than in other markets (Colombia and Mexico).
  • Maximized reach and adoption through the synergy of ATL and digital marketing strategies.
  • Strengthened SEO performance and brand authority through ATL-driven branded searches and media backlinks.
  • Positioned Dr. Sol as a competitive player in the Peruvian loan market with growing market share.
