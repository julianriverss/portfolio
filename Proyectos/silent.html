Project Journey

Challenge

Xlab wanted to build visibility in Latin America but had near-zero Google presence for its target market. The company needed a complete SEO foundation to begin appearing in relevant searches and establish a digital footprint in the region.

Strategy

Establish strong SEO fundamentals to unlock indexing, relevance, and crawl efficiency:

  • Technical hygiene + CWV-oriented performance (especially image weight).
  • Keyword research focused on LATAM intent (core, supporting, long-tail).
  • Content plan aligned to search demand and product fit.
  • Proper indexation and internal linking to surface priority pages.

Execution

  • Compressed/served images responsibly (next-gen formats, dimensions, lazy load) to cut page weight.
  • Built a tiered keyword map and produced/optimized content for primary & secondary intents.
  • Created and submitted XML sitemap; fixed robots/meta directives; reviewed internal/external links.
  • Set up and configured Google Search Console (properties, sitemaps, coverage, enhancements) and implemented monitoring.

Results

  • Went from no meaningful Google visibility to an average position ≈ 12 across target queries for the LATAM market.
  • Improved crawlability and load performance, enabling broader index coverage and consistent impressions.
  • Established SEO foundation for continued growth and expansion in the Latin American market.
