Performance Reporting

Multi-Channel Marketing Templates

Project Overview

A suite of customizable reporting templates that provide companies with a centralized view of performance across digital marketing channels, enabling faster decision-making and strategy optimization.

Services:
Digital Marketing Analytics
Channels:
SEO, Social Media, Advertising
Scope:
Dashboard Design, Data Integration, Visualization

Project Journey

Challenge

Companies struggled with fragmented reporting across different marketing channels, making it difficult to gain a holistic view of performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions efficiently. Marketing teams spent excessive time creating reports rather than acting on insights.

Approach

  • Built interactive dashboards where users can navigate between different reports by clicking buttons.
  • Designed reports with two layers:
    • General overview: high-level KPIs, growth, and trends.
    • Specific breakdowns: campaign-level or platform-level details.
  • Adapted each template to the unique needs of the company, ensuring flexibility and scalability.

Execution

  • Delivered SEO, Social Media, and Advertising reports that can be viewed separately or in combination.
  • Integrated API connections for real-time data (when companies had subscriptions to connector platforms).
  • Ensured templates were easy to navigate and visually consistent across channels.

Results

  • Provided marketing teams with clear, centralized insights for faster decision-making.
  • Increased efficiency by reducing time spent on manual reporting.
  • Enabled executives to compare strategies across channels and identify optimization opportunities.
