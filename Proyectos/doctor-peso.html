DOCTOR PESO COLOMBIA

FinTech - Financial Services

Project Overview

Developed a multi-channel growth strategy for Dr. Peso Colombia, optimizing acquisition costs while expanding reach through digital and offline channels, achieving significant improvements in key performance metrics.

Client:
Digital Finance International - Doctor Peso Colombia
Duration:
14 months
Role:
Growth Marketing Lead

Project Journey

Challenge

Dr. Peso needed to reduce acquisition costs, diversify channels beyond Facebook, and strengthen its brand presence in both digital and offline spaces.

Strategy

Design a multi-channel growth plan combining performance optimization, new acquisition channels, influencer and content strategies, and technical improvements to the website:

  • Paid Media Optimization: Reduce Facebook Ads CPL and test new creative/segmentation strategies.
  • Channel Expansion: Open new digital channels such as TikTok Ads (via international agency), Threads, Instagram content, and influencer campaigns.
  • SEO & Content: Implement technical improvements (Core Web Vitals) and content strategies to capture organic demand and boost brand authority.
  • ATL Exploration: Lead research and negotiations with agencies to explore billboards, metro, metro cable, and shopping centers for brand visibility.

Execution

  • Reduced CPL in Facebook Ads from $20 → $15 through campaign and creative optimization (using funnel metrics like CPC, CTR, CPM).
  • Activated influencer campaigns and expanded content to Threads and Instagram, strengthening awareness and reach.
  • Implemented SEO technical fixes and a new content strategy, driving +30% organic traffic, domain authority growth to 25 DA, and +1,800 clicks.
  • Pioneered TikTok Ads in Colombia by partnering with a Vietnam-based agency, testing campaigns before the platform was officially available locally.
  • Researched ATL opportunities (billboards, metro, shopping centers) and led negotiations with agencies to evaluate feasibility.

Results

  • 25% lower CPL on Facebook Ads.
  • +30% organic traffic and 1,800 extra clicks, increasing brand recognition.
  • Validated TikTok Ads as a viable future channel for acquisition in Colombia.
  • Built a multi-channel framework that positioned Dr. Peso for scalable growth, integrating digital, influencer, and offline strategies.
