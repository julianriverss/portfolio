Vive Meals

Meal Preparation & Delivery Service - 2019

Project Overview

A complete digital marketing strategy for Vive Meals, a startup focused on healthy meal preparation and delivery. The project included developing the company's online presence, implementing growth tactics, and creating a sustainable customer acquisition strategy.

Client:
Vive Meals
Duration:
9 months (2019)
Role:
Digital Marketing Strategist

Project Journey

Challenge

Vive Meals (Miami) was plateaued at a steady but stagnant customer base (~$20K/month) and lacked the systems to re-engage buyers, scale demand, or forecast production.

Strategy

Build a full growth stack to drive repeat purchases and controlled scale:

  • CRM + lifecycle automation for remarketing, win-backs, and upsells.
  • Offer architecture (bundles, trials, subscriptions) to lift AOV and retention.
  • Paid + referral engine with influencers to drive top-of-funnel demand.
  • Ops/marketing alignment to match demand generation with production capacity.

Execution

  • Implemented a lightweight CRM & automation (segmentation, email/SMS flows, cart recovery, churn prevention).
  • Launched remarketing & promo calendars (new menu drops, limited offers).
  • Ran influencer & referral programs to generate UGC and social proof.
  • Coordinated with the kitchen to phase demand and redesign the production process once orders spiked (capacity planning, cut-off windows, batching).

Results

  • Demand surge so strong the team temporarily paused sales to catch up on fulfillment.
  • Revenue scaled from ~$20K → ~$60K per month after system adoption.
  • Instagram community grew to ~80,000 followers via the referral/influencer strategy.
  • Stronger brand equity and recurring revenue; later rebranded to Nutri Vive.
